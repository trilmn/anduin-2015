Product Designer

Anduin Transactions is a groundbreaking new financial technology startup founded by Joe Lonsdale, co-founder of Palantir Technologies and Addepar. As leaders and engineers at Palantir Technologies, One King's Lane, IBM, WalmartLabs and imo.im, the founding team was deeply ingrained in Silicon Valley best practices and methodologies. Our mission is to create a universal standard for tracking and managing transactions in financial markets. We are building a world-class team in San Francisco and Ho Chi Minh City and are backed by top-tier industry influencers and insiders.

Anduin is looking for a Product Designer who wants to move fast, and be the first to solve intractable real world problems through design. We want our people to be bold, to dream big, to take risks and to learn from trying (and failing).

  • Position Type: Full time
  • Location: Ho Chi Minh City

You will be a great fit for us if:

  • You have the will and grit to push past tough obstacles and help build a meaningful company from the ground up.
  • You have the curiosity to explore domains outside your own and the instinct to leverage radical solutions to make our users successful.
  • You have an innate passion and eye for design; and keep up on the latest techniques and advances.
  • You can think independently and critically–going beyond design to deeply consider the user experience and product workflow.
  • You are not afraid to learn technical skills further downstream from mockups–even venturing into front end implementation.

Basic Qualifications

  • Designing experiences that can solve the product problem, not just some aesthetic aspect.
  • Prototyping & Implementing your designs directly in the product, you should know clearly what you're achieving.
  • Working with the rest of the team to help elevate the product.
  • Communicate design effectively to anyone in the team.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Fluently translate your design into clean & semantic HTML/CSS.
  • Having knowledge in writing Javascript, Angular JS, React JS or willingly to learn if you don't know any of them.
  • Having knowledge in interactive design, UI animation.
  • Experience with startup environment.
  • Experience in designing enterprise product.

Benefits

  • We will match exceptional talent with exceptional compensation (salary and equity).
  • Receive top-of-the-line 15-inch Macbook Pro and 27-inch Thunderbolt Display.
  • You can shape the company culture where the best ideas always win out–regardless of the role, title or seniority.

Making Private Transactions
Quicker, Simpler and Repeatable

Anduin Transactions is a technology startup founded by Alin Bui & Joe Lonsdale (co-founder of Palantir Technologies and Addepar, and GP at 8VC) with the vision to streamline transaction workflow in financial markets.

Backed by top-tier industry influencers and insiders, we are building a world-class team with the best engineers across the U.S. and Asia.

A Smarter Standard

Our mission is to create pleasurable-to-use, viral systems that transform the private share ecosystem in a way that informs and protects entrepreneurs, engineers, and investors. We believe that smart technology provides the solution to make these complex, variable transactions simpler, quicker and repeatable. Our action-biased, highly customizable automated platform aims to be an industry standard for multi-party collaboration and deal completion.

 

Concept and Execution

Financial transactions in private markets are opaque, non-deterministic, and unstandardized. We deconstruct the back-and-forth mechanics behind each transaction; analyze pressure points in the workflow; and conceptualize holistic systems that must anticipate human behavior in any number of cases. This is the challenge we meet with technology that is predictive, adaptive, contextual and highly secure.
